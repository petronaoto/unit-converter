Operations Manual (Version 1.6)

1. Quick Introduction

The O&G Engineering Converter is designed for speed and accuracy in high-stakes environments. The system operates on two levels:

  • Standard Operations: Pre-configured modules (Gas Volume, Pressure, etc.) are always available on the General tab for immediate use.
  • Configure Module: An advanced generator that allows you to instantly spin up customized conversion cards. New in V1.6: Custom modules are now automatically saved to your browser's local memory!

2. Using Standard Operations & Copying Values

Values entered in either the top or bottom input fields will automatically calculate the corresponding equivalent. You can swap magnitude scales on the fly using the right-side dropdowns. New in V1.6: Click the clipboard icon to instantly copy any value!

3. Initializing a Custom Module

When you need a specific conversion not listed in the default cards (such as converting Liquid Volume to Mass), locate the "INITIALIZE NEW MODULE" button at the bottom of the General tab.

CLICK TO INITIALIZE

4. Configuring the Parameters

Clicking the initialize button opens the Configuration Modal. You have two choices here:

  • Quick Load Template: Select a pre-configured option from the top dropdown (e.g., "Dynamic Viscosity"). This auto-fills the rest of the form.
  • Custom Conversion: Leave the template dropdown blank, name your module, define Unit 1 and Unit 2, and provide the exact mathematical multiplier.
